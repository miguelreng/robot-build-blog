# Computer Vision Improvements for Sidewalk Analysis

At Robot.com, we are continuously refining how our systems perceive the world. Our latest iteration focuses on the sidewalk analysis pipeline in Arlington, specifically addressing image quality and defect segmentations.

## Smart Quality Checks

Before we even attempt to detect defects, we now apply OpenCV-based quality filters. By analyzing blur, texture, and edge density, the system automatically rejects captures where the sidewalk isn't clearly visible. This dramatically reduces noise in our dataset.
